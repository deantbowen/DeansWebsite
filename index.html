About me

I'm Dean, a Cybersecurity Analyst with over a decade of experience in IT. My passion for computers started early—around the age of 8.

I began my career as a PC Technician, building computers and troubleshooting desktops and laptop challenges.

I've also been an IT Specialist, fixing and deploying network devices and machines.

My journey continued as a Data Centre Technician at a data center company, providing remote hands for DC clients, networking, and troubleshooting Linux environments.

Currently, I'm rocking it as a Cyber Security Analyst II at a cybersecurity firm, where I dive deep into Network Security Auditing, Incident Response, System Hardening, and keeping our systems safe with IDS/IPS.

Before this role, I gained valuable experience as a Cyber Security Analyst I, specializing in Security Awareness, Endpoint Detection and Response (EDR), and Security Information and Event Management (SIEM).

These areas allowed me to delve deep into cybersecurity practices and solutions.

Throughout my career, I've kept up with the ever-changing world of cybersecurity, always eager to learn and apply new skills.
